About Me

Engineering, Character & Vision

I am Fitra Ramadhani, an Electrical Engineering graduate from Universitas Hasanuddin, specializing in Telecommunication Engineering. During my academic journey, I successfully executed numerous technical projects ranging from wireless system design to RF simulations as part of my rigorous academic assignments.

My core expertise lies in RF Design and Antenna Simulation. I am proficient in using industry-standard tools like CST Studio Suite to design innovative solutions, including my latest research on a dual-band microstrip antenna for wearable radar applications.

Beyond my technical skills, I am a highly adaptive individual with a relentless curiosity for emerging technologies. I embrace new challenges as opportunities for growth and take pride in being a fast learner who is always ready to evolve alongside the industry.

My career objective is to contribute professionally to the advancement of telecommunication infrastructure and wireless innovation. I aim to leverage my expertise to develop efficient, next-generation connectivity solutions and drive meaningful progress in the global telecommunications landscape.
